Further than mere highlands, Puno has some natural secrets.</span></p><p><span style="font-weight: 400;">At over 12,000 feet above sea level, Puno is on a high plateau. These ecosystems are unique due to their high altitude and climate. </span></p><p><span style="font-weight: 400;">The climate around Lake Titicaca is cold and semi-arid. These conditions create a special environment for various plants and animals.</span></p><p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Vegetation around Lake Titicaca includes totora reeds, which are essential to the ecosystem.
