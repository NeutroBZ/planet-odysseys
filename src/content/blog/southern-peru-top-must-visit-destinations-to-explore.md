<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">If you plan to visit Peru, these are the unmissable destinations. Each destination offers a unique charm. The possibilities for adventure are great.</span></p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Join us as we uncover the best attractions in the south of the country. Whether you're a nature lover or history buff, there's something for you. Let's dive into these must-see spots!</span></p>

<p><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Machu_Picchu"><b>Machu Picchu</b></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> is a breathtaking destination in Southern Peru.  More specifically in the Cusco region. And possibly the most famous recommendation.</span></p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">It is an ancient Inca city located on top of a mountain in the Peruvian jungle. The ruins are beautiful. It is a well preserved city made of rock. Visitors can explore its intricate structures. The Lost City of the Incas is an impressive site.</span></p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Machu Picchu is a must-visit in general. It is a vision of the ancient world and its true magnitude. The smaller Inca ruins can blind side you on the true scale of the Inca empire.</span></p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Visitors can immerse into the grandeur of the city. The nature around it is almost a part of it. It is just a unique experience. For more explorations you have a lot of alternatives to hike to Machu Picchu.</span></p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">The Colca Canyon is a spectacular destination in Southern Peru. It is famous for its depth. Even comparing it on a worldwide scale. Travelers will find breathtaking landscapes. </span></p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">In the Arequipa Region. The Colca Canyon offers visitors not only nature. But a cultural experience along small towns. The possibility of discovering andean nature. And more.</span></p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">The Colca Canyon is a must-see. Its dramatic scenery captivates adventurers. Experience the natural beauty and cultural richness of Arequipa. </span></p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">The Nazca Lines are an enigmatic attraction in Southern Peru. These ancient geoglyphs stretch across the arid Nazca Desert. The lines depict various animals, plants, and geometric shapes. The purpose of these massive designs remains a mystery. </span></p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">The best way to appreciate the Nazca geoglyphs is from the air. Flights over the desert provide an optimal viewpoint.</span></p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">The Nazca Lines are a must-visit. These lines come from a civilization more ancient than the Incas. And the prediction they had is amazing. </span></p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">The effort put into them must not be forgotten. They could so easily be erased by the desert. The Nazca Lines are an impressive reminder of the multiple civilizations that populated Peru.</span></p>
