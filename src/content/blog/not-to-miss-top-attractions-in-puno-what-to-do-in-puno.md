<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Beyond the city center, Puno's landscape has ancient ruins that speak to its pre-Inca and Inca heritage. <p><span style="font-weight: 400;">The site is home to a large statue of a condor, a sacred bird in Andean culture, symbolizing the connection between earth and sky.
